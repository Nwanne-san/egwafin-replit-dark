## **Design System & Visual Identity**

**Color Palette:**
- **Primary Background**: Deep matte black (`#0a0a0a` to `#0f0f0f`) for the main canvas, creating a premium, modern financial aesthetic
- **Accent Color**: Vibrant lime/neon green (`#a3e635` or `#84cc16`) for CTAs, highlights, and interactive elements—symbolizing growth, prosperity, and financial vitality
- **Secondary Background**: Charcoal gray (`#1a1a1a`, `#262626`) for cards, sections, and elevated surfaces
- **Text Primary**: Pure white (`#ffffff`) for headings and important content
- **Text Secondary**: Muted gray (`#a1a1aa`, `#71717a`) for body text and descriptions
- **Border Color**: Subtle gray (`#27272a`) for card outlines and dividers
- **Success/Light Section**: Bright lime green background (`#a3e635`) used sparingly for high-impact CTA sections (like the contact area)

**Typography:**
- **Font Family**: Modern geometric sans-serif (Inter, Plus Jakarta Sans, or Satoshi)—clean, professional, highly legible for financial content
- **Headings**: Bold/Extra-bold weight (700-800), tight letter-spacing (-0.02em), large scale hierarchy
- **Hero Title**: 56px-72px, line-height 1.1, maximum 6 words per line
- **Body Text**: 16px-18px, line-height 1.6, regular weight (400-500)
- **Navigation**: 14px-15px, medium weight, uppercase or capitalize
- **Stats/Numbers**: Large display font (48px-64px), bold, tight tracking

**Visual Elements:**
- **Border Radius**: Consistent large rounding—buttons (24px-32px pill shape), cards (16px-24px), images (12px-16px)
- **Spacing**: Generous whitespace (80px-120px) between sections; contained max-width (1280px-1440px) with 16px-48px padding
- **Shadows**: Subtle dark shadows (`0 4px 20px rgba(0,0,0,0.3)`) for floating elements and cards
- **Graphics**: Abstract lime-green starburst/sparkle icons (4-pointed stars) used as decorative accents near headings

## **Animations & Interactions**

**Micro-interactions:**
- **Buttons**: On hover, fill animation sweeps from left (lime to white, or white to lime), text color inverts, slight scale (1.02)
- **Links**: Underline grows from center on hover (lime color)
- **Cards**: Subtle lift (transform: translateY(-4px)) + shadow increase on hover
- **Accordion**: Smooth height transition (300ms ease-in-out), arrow rotates 90deg when open

**Scroll Animations:**
- **Reveal**: Elements fade in + translateY (20px to 0) as they enter viewport (Intersection Observer, threshold 0.1)
- **Stagger**: Grid items animate in sequence (100ms delay between each)
- **Parallax**: Hero image moves at 0.8x scroll speed; background shapes move at 0.5x

**Special Effects:**
- **Starburst Icons**: Slow rotation (20s infinite linear) or pulse scale animation
- **Gradient Orbs**: Subtle floating animation (up/down 20px, 6s ease-in-out infinite) positioned behind content for depth
- **Image Reveal**: Images load with a clip-path reveal from bottom to top or circular expand

**Navigation:**
- **Active State**: Current page link has lime green dot indicator below or lime text color
- **Mobile Menu**: Slide in from right (transform: translateX(100%) to 0), backdrop blur on main content

## **Responsive Behavior**

**Desktop (1280px+)**: Full layout as described, side-by-side columns
**Tablet (768px-1279px)**: 
- Hero stacks vertically (image below text)
- Stats become 2x2 grid
- Services accordion becomes full width, image below
**Mobile (< 768px)**:
- Single column layouts
- Navigation collapses to hamburger
- Marquee text size reduces to 32px
- Contact section stacks vertically (form below info)
- Increased padding (16px-24px sides)

**Technical Notes:**
- Use CSS Grid and Flexbox
- Container max-width: 1280px (xl) or 1440px (2xl)
- Implement smooth scrolling (scroll-behavior: smooth)
- Ensure focus states for accessibility (lime green outline)
- Loading state: Skeleton screens matching the dark theme
